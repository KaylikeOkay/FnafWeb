Songs

There are many Fnaf songs that where made over the year. They are so popular that most people have heard them without knowing it. Some of the artist who made the most of these is 'The Living Tombstone' who made 'It`s Been So Long' , which is about the mom regretting losing her child which got killed by William aka Purple guy out of her eyes, 'Five Nights at Freddy's' , which is about FNAF in general, 'Die In a Fire' , which is about William aka Purple guy who tries to destroy the animatronics and 'I Got No Time' , which is from the perspective of the player in Fnaf 4.The songs have around 122.513.665 (It's Been So Long) and 90.643.046 (Five Nights at Freddy's) plays on Spotify and is with that two of the most played songs of them. Other popular songs are :

  • 'Just Gold' by MandoPony
  • 'Survive the Night' by MandoPony,
  • 'STAY CALM' by Griffinilla
  • 'BREAK MY MIND' by Dagames

Of course there are so many more songs from the fandom but these are the one that most people know and as you can see many of them are made by The Living Tombstone, they also made songs for many other fandoms.
